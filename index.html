Welcome to Better Bike ABQ

This site hopes to inform both recreational and commuter rides alike of the riding dangers Areas in Albuquerque.
As a bicycle enthusiast it was my personal interest that gave the spark for this map. This project started as a
class project and grew into a website as I wanted more and more information about the issue. The question seemed
simple, where are the safest roads in Albuquerque to ride a bike? With Much help from Jennifer Buntz of the Duke
City Wheelmen I was able to obtain some accident data gathered by the NMDOT. Using this data I located the accidents
on a map of Albuquerque in the hope that safe biking paths in the city would be obvious. What I discovered was there
are many areas of town that boost a high incident of accidents. The factors for these accidents are many, as it is
difficult to surmise only one definitive contributing factor from the data. This site hopes to highlight Danger
Zones in the ABQ metro area as well as provide some gathered advice for best riding practices in the city. This site
hopes to improve ridership as a whole and to keep both riders and drivers safe as both have a responsibility to watch
out for the other.
