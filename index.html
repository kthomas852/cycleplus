Welcome to Badcompass

Just a group of people that probably should have grown up a long time ago but...you know. Anyway, we like our lifestyle
and can't really seem to make it through the year without planning a few epic adventures. If you are like us or are just
getting into climbing, cycling and or hiking and need some ideas or just a little advise then this is the site for you.
We have stories from all walks of life from the newly graduated PHD looking for a an adrenaline fix to the local bike
mechanics that just love to ride. Let us be your free guides, in the New Mexico area and beyond. Enjoy our stories
and feel free to tell us yours. The members of badcompass do all their own stunts and serve as each other's medics,
support team and camera crew. So come join the lifestyle; get inspired and always stay the course!
